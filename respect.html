Respect Others Online

Respecting Others Online means treating people with kindness, politeness, and understanding while using social media, emails, or gaming platforms. The internet connects people from different cultures and backgrounds, so it is important to respect different opinions. Avoid cyberbullying, spreading false rumors, or posting hurtful comments. Practicing respectful communication helps create positive online communities where everyone feels safe and valued.
