Avoid Plagiarism

Plagiarism means copying someone else’s work, ideas, or content and claiming it as your own. With easy access to information online, many people are tempted to copy and paste without giving proper credit. This is dishonest and unfair to the original creator. Cyber ethics encourages originality, honesty, and respect for intellectual property. Creating your own content builds creativity and confidence.
